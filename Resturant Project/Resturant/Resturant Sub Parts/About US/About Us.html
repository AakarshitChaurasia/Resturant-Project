What is A Restaurant

A Restaurant is an establishment which prepares and and drink to customers in return for money, either paid before the meal, after the meal, or with a running tab. Meals are generally served and eaten
on premises, but many restaurants also offer takeout and food delivery services. Restaurants vary greatly in appearance and offerings, including a wide variety of the main chef's cuisines and
service models.
